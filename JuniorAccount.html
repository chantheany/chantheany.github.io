Junior Account

As parents, we know it is never too early to start saving for your child’s future. TCH Bank’s Junior Account is designed to help you save for your child’s future. The money invested in the account is held for the child until age 18.

Account features at a glance

  • Available in US Dollar.
  • Enjoy a premium interest rate.
  • Free Passbook for your transaction records.
  • Money invested is held for the child until age 18.
  • Special Offer to Parent/Guardian
  • Credit card facility up to 80% of deposited balance.

Conditions

  • Available for child age 15 years and below.

    • Secure Your Loved One’s Future with TCH Bank

    TCH Bank launched the Junior Account with both the child’s and the parent’s interests in mind. The parent/guardian operates the account for the child to ensure the child’s savings are generating good returns safely.

    Start Savings for Your Loved One Today

    Visit any of our branches located nearest to you and bring along the following documents for the application:
  • Child: Birth Certificate and 2 Passport-sized photos.
  • Parent/Guardian: Identification Document or Passport.
  • Initial minimum deposit of USD 200.
