The benefits of TCH Fixed Deposit lie in its flexibility. You can choose the tenure of your savings and how you want the interest to be paid, be it monthly or at maturity. Whichever tenure you choose, you can be confident that you will receive a competitive interest rate on your deposit.

Account Requirement

  • Available in 4 currencies: Khmer Riel (KHR), US Dollar (USD), Thai Baht (THB) and Chinese Yuan Renminbi (RMB).
  • Flexible tenure of 1 month, 2 months, 3 months, 6 months, 9 months, 12 months and 24 months.
  • Enjoy attractive rates on your deposit based on the tenure you choose.
  • Interest is automatically credited to your Savings or Current Account.
  • Enjoy flexibility of collecting the interest payment, either monthly or at maturity.
  • Convenient auto-renewal option.
  • Credit card facility up to 80% of deposited balance.

Conditions

  • Available for individuals age 18 years and over.
  • Available for Corporate.
  • Currently have a Savings Account or Current Account with the Bank.
