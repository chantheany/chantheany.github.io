Current Account

Current account is available to be opened in 3 currencies: Khmer Riel (KHR), US Dollar (USD), and Thai Baht (THB); with an initial deposit of US$ 200 or foreign currency equivalent. A current account is particularly useful to facilitate business transactions by using checks and transfers in lieu of cash. It can be used together with a Savings account for check clearance by transferring funds between accounts.

Account features at a glance

  • Available in 3 currencies: Khmer Riel (KHR), US Dollar (USD), and Thai Baht (THB).
  • No monthly keeping fee.
  • Free monthly bank statement.
  • Chequebook facility.
  • Easy access to your money through Internet and Mobile banking facilities, Canadia ATM terminals, and Easycash ATM network.
  • Bill payment (water, electricity) facility over the
  • counter or via our ATM terminals.

Conditions

  • Available for individuals age 18 years and over.
  • Available for Corporate.
